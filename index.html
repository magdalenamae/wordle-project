WORDLE

Enter a Five letter Word

RULES

You have 6 guesses to guess the correct word. You can guess any valid word but it must not contain double letters. After each guess, each letter will turn red, yellow, or gray.

  • RED is the right letter in right spot

  • YELLOW is the right letter in wrong spot

  • GREY is the wrong letter
